Tableau Cloud vs Tableau Next

Know the product, the buyer, and the pitch

Tableau Cloud
Enterprise BI · SaaS · AI-Enhanced Analytics
What it is

The fully managed SaaS version of Tableau. Includes Tableau Agent — describe what you want, it builds the chart (Einstein Trust Layer) — and Pulse for proactive metric monitoring with AI explanations. No infrastructure to manage.

Use cases
  • Migrating from Tableau Server / on-prem
  • Enterprise dashboards and governed self-service BI
  • Centralized IT control over data and access
  • Pulse: metric alerts and AI-explained anomalies
  • Tableau Agent: build charts from plain-English descriptions
  • Complex multi-source data analysis
Who buys it
IT Director Head of Analytics BI Lead CDO CTO

Centralized teams responsible for data governance, infrastructure, and analytics operations.

Salesforce connection
  • Part of the Salesforce portfolio — same AE motion
  • Native connector to Salesforce data
  • Governed analytics for data outside the CRM
Tableau Next
AI-First · Conversational · Salesforce-Native
What it is

The next generation of Tableau — conversational AI analytics built natively on Salesforce. Users ask questions and get intelligent answers powered by Einstein AI. No dashboard required.

Use cases
  • Natural language Q&A on business data
  • AI agents that proactively surface insights
  • Analytics embedded natively inside Salesforce
  • Business users who find dashboards too slow or complex
  • Einstein + Agentforce analytics workflows
  • Conversational analytics for non-technical users
Who buys it
CRO CMO VP of Sales COO Salesforce Admin

Business leaders and Salesforce-first buyers who want faster answers — not a BI build project.

Salesforce connection
  • Built on Salesforce — not just connected to it
  • Embedded inside Salesforce orgs and flows
  • Powered by Einstein AI — shares the same data layer
  • Works with Agentforce for autonomous analytics actions
